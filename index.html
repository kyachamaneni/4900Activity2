UNO provides shuttle services to the students between the Dodge and the Scott Campuses. The students can make use of this shuttle services and can save considerable amount on transportation and also time for looking the parking area. UNO shuttle services are operated by considering the weather conditions in Omaha. The students can also track the current status of the shuttles by installing the UNO Shuttle Tracker app.
