BLUE ROUTE - MAMMEL HALL / CRISS LIBRARY SHUTTLE

  • Fall/spring when classes are in session, operates 6:30 A.M. to 12 A.M. (M-F)
  • Buses arrive approx. every 8 minutes from 7 A.M. to 6 P.M. on a regular class days
  • From 6 P.M. to 9 P.M., bus will arrive every 18 minutes
  • From 9 P.M. to 12 A.M. a floater will service this route every 20 minutes.
  • Routes may be slower the first two weeks of fall/spring semesters, allow extra time
  • Schedules subject to change

ORANGE ROUTE - SCOTT CROSSING / CRISS LIBRARY SHUTTLE

  • Fall/Spring when classes are in session, operates 5:30 A.M. to 12 A.M. (M-F)
  • Buses arrive approx. every 7 minutes from 7 A.M. to 6 P.M. on a regular class days
  • From 6 P.M. to 9 P.M., bus will arrive every 18 minutes
  • From 9 P.M. to 12 A.M. a floater will service this route every 20 minutes.
  • Routes may be slower the first two weeks of fall/spring semesters, allow extra time
  • Not available during the summer

GREEN ROUTE - PACIFIC ST GARAGE / LOT M SHUTTLE

  • Fall/Spring when classes are in session, operates 7 A.M. to 7 P.M. (M-F)
  • Buses arrive approx. every 8 minutes on a regular class day
  • Routes may be slower the first two weeks of fall/spring semesters, allow extra time
  • Not available during the summer

PINK ROUTE - NEWMAN CENTER / LOT N SHUTTLE

  • Buses arrive approx. every 15 minutes
  • Fall/spring when classes are in session, operates 7 A.M.-1 P.M. and 3 P.M.-6 P.M. (M-Th)
  • Not available during the summer

BROWN ROUTE - SCOTT SCHOLARS (Scott Hall) / CRISS LIBRARY SHUTTLE

  • Fall/Spring when classes are in session
  • Buses arrive approx. every 15-20 minutes
  • Routes may be slower the first two weeks of fall/spring semesters, allow extra time
  • Not available during the summer

PURPLE ROUTE - LIMITED SERIVCE

  • Runs during Summer, Fall Break, Winter Break and Spring Break
  • Stops at Mammel Hall, Scott Crossing, Pacific St. Garage and Criss Library
  • Buses arrive approx. every 15-20 minutes
  • Operates 7 A.M. to 7 P.M. (M-F)
